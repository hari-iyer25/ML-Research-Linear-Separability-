Linearly Separable Sat Problems:

4-Boolean Tautology:
1. (A or B or C or D or not A)
2. (A or not A) and (B or not B) and (C or not C) and (D or not D)
3. (A or B) or (B or C) or (C or D) or (not B) 

4-bool UN-SAT:
1. (A and B and C and not D) 
2. (A or B) and (B and not B) and (C and D) 
3. (B or C) or (A and D) and ((not B and not D and not A))
